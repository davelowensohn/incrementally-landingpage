About

Here are just some of the features we've packed into IncremenTally:

  • Multiple counters with adjustable increments.
  • Custom counter names with preloaded defaults.
  • Full command history with infinite levels of undo.
  • Beautiful, intuitive interface.
  • Usability options for lefties and the visually impaired.
